RIVERA LABS BETA TESTER NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This is an agreement, effective, between Rivera Labs (hereinafter referred to as the "Developer") and _______________ (hereinafter referred to as the "Tester"), in which Tester agrees to test a prototype IOS Application known as _________________ (hereinafter referred to as the "App”) and to keep the Developer aware and informed of the test results.

I. Developer Obligations

Developer shall provide Tester with a copy of the App and any necessary documentation, and/or any required instructions, to Tester on how to use it and what test data is desired by Developer. Upon satisfactory completion of the testing, Developer shall furnish Tester with one free copy of the IOS App, contingent upon Developer's decision to proceed with its production. Developer will inform the Tester when the App is approved by the iStore.

II. Beta Tester Obligations

Tester shall test the App under normally expected operating conditions in Tester's IOS device. Tester shall gather and report test data as agreed upon with Developer. Tester shall allow Developer access to the App during a previously scheduled meeting for inspection, modifications and maintenance. Once the Beta Testing period expires, Tester agrees to publish a review of the App on its blog and/or website.

III. Trade Secret

The App contains valuable, confidential, trade secret information owned by Developer. It is entirely owned, and a valuable trade secret of, by the Developer. It is entrusted to Tester only for the purpose set forth in this Agreement. Tester shall maintain App in the strictest confidence. Tester will not, without Developer's prior written consent:

  1. disclose any information about the App, its design and performance specifications, its code, and the existence of the beta test and its results to anyone other than the Developer or Tester's employees who are performing the testing and who shall be subject to nondisclosure restrictions at least as protective as those set forth in this Agreement;
  2. modify, copy, duplicate any portion of the App or its related documentation, except to the extent necessary to perform beta testing or reverse engineer, decompile or disassemble the App or any portion of it.
  3. During this Agreement and for a period of 1 year thereafter, Tester agrees that he/she will not, without the express prior written consent of Developer, disclose any Confidential Information or any part thereof to any third party.

A. Recipient also agrees that it shall not duplicate, translate, modify, copy, printout, disassemble, decompile or otherwise tamper with the Product or any firmware, circuit board or software provided therewith.

IV. Security

Tester shall take reasonable security precautions to prevent App from being seen by unauthorized individuals whether stored on Tester's iPhone, hard drive or on physical copies such as CD-ROMS, USB Drives or any other type of media. Tester shall lock all documents, and its associated documentation, in a desk or file cabinet when not in use.

V. Terms of the Agreement

The test period shall last from _________,___ until _________,___. This Agreement shall terminate at the end of the test period or when Developer asks Tester to return the App, whichever occurs first. The restrictions and obligations contained in Clauses IV, VII, VIII, IX and X shall survive the expiration, termination or cancellation of this Agreement, and shall continue to bind Tester, its successors, heirs and assigns.

VI. Return of App Related Documentation

Upon the conclusion of the testing period, or at Developer's request, Tester shall within 10 days return the original, and all copies of the App’s related materials to Developer and delete all portions the same from computer memory and from its IOS device.
